Anders Hejlsberg, creator of TypeScript (and C# and Turbo Pascal), designed TypeScript with one goal: **make developers more productive, not more bureaucratic**.

## What TypeScript gives you

1. **Intelligent autocomplete**: `user.` shows you all properties
2. **Compile-time errors**: Don't wait for users to report bugs
3. **Safe refactoring**: Renaming a function updates all code

## Final reflection

The time you "lose" writing types you recover multiplied in less debugging, better documentation, and fearless refactoring.
