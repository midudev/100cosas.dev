Kent C. Dodds has fought against dogmatism: **what works for Google doesn't necessarily work for your 3-person startup**.

## The right question

Don't ask "What's the best practice?"

Ask "What's the best practice **for my context**?"

## Final reflection

Dogmas are comfortable because they avoid thinking. But good engineering is exactly that: thinking about the specific context.
