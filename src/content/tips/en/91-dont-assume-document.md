Sarah Drasner promotes documenting decisions, not just code: **the "why" is more valuable than the "what"**.

## ADRs: Architecture Decision Records

Document why decisions were made, not just what they were.

## Final reflection

In 2 years, no one will remember why PostgreSQL was chosen. But if it's documented, the next team won't repeat the investigation.
